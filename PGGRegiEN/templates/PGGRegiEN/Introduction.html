Introduction 

This is an online experiment in decision-making, in which you can earn money. The amount you earn will depend on your decisions and the decisions of others.

Groups 

In this experiment you have been randomly assigned to a group of 5 people. Each group will remain together throughout the experiment. 

Rounds 

The experiment will consist of 25 identical rounds and in each of these rounds you will earn money. How much depends on the choices you and the others in your group make. Once every member of the group has made the decision, you will be redirected to the results page where you can examine the behaviour of others in your group. 

Income 

At the beginning of each round, each of you will receive 55 units of experimental currency which we will call “points”, 55 points each round are called your “income”. Each point is worth approximately 0.065 DKK. Your income is therefore worth 3.6 DKK in each of the 25 rounds.

Decision 

In each round, after receiving your 55 points, you may choose to keep them to yourself or to contribute some or all of them to a group project. You may contribute any amount from zero up to your entire income. Contributions higher than 55 points will not be accepted. 

Each player in your group has the same income as you and the same decision to make. 

If the sum of the contributions of the persons in your group meets or exceeds the threshold level of 125 points, the group will receive a reward which is called a “bonus” the value of which is 250 points. Everybody in the group gets an equal share of the bonus, that is 50 points (about 3.2 DKK), regardless of how much they contributed. Your total income for the period will be your initial income, plus the bonus, minus your contribution. If the sum of the contributions is less than the threshold level of 125 points, the additional points will not be provided. In this event, the contributions are returned to their owners, and your total income for the period will simply be your original income of 55 points.  

Earnings 

Regardless of your performance you will receive at least 40 DKK for your participation in this study.
Depending on yours and others’ decisions you can make up to 170 DKK in total. Average earnings for participants are expected to be 110 DKK. 

In summary, if total contributions in your group equal 125 points or more and you have contributed X, your earnings from this round will be:  Your earnings = your income (55 points; 3.6 DKK)  – your contribution(X points ) + additional points (50 points; 3.2 DKK) 

If, on the other hand, total contributions in your group equal less than 125 points and you have contributed X, your earnings will simply be your income because of the full refund rule:  Your earnings = your income (55 points; 3.6DKK) 

Your total earnings consist of the sum of your earnings from the 25 paid rounds. 

Remember that each token is worth approximately 0.065 DKK.

Example A

You contribute 26 points towards the public good. The other four players contribute a total of 97 points to the public account. The total group contribution is therefore 123 points and the threshold of 125 is not reached and the points you contributed are returned. Your earnings for this round are therefore the same as your initial income, 55 points.

Example B

You contribute 37 points towards the public good. The other four players contribute a total of 97 points in the public good. The total group contribution is therefore more than 125, the threshold is reached and the group earns a bonus of 250 points. The bonus is equally shared between all five members of the group. To compute your earnings for this round we start with your initial income, 55 points, subtract your contribution of 37 points and finally add your share of the bonus which is 50 points. Your total earnings for this round are consequently 68 points.
