Welcome to this experiment!

In this study, you will be playing together with two other players, who are chosen randomly from all participants.

This study involves two separate games. The first will be a dice roll game, and the second will resemble a joint project. Note: your performance in the first game will carry over to the second game.

Your overall performance will also determine your overall pay!

You are awarded with a show up fee of {{ show_up_fee_kr }}. You will keep all earnings you will make above that.
