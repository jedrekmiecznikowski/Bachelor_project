Now, you will play a second game, which models a joined project.
Important: you will play together with the same people you have played with before.

In this game, your group has the opportunity to undertake a joint project. Each participant in the group decides how much she or he is going to contribute to the project.

The amount you can contribute depends on your group's performance in the previous game. During the last game, you have earned {{ cumulative_payoff }}. In addition to that, you are also given a further endowment of {{ Constants.endowment }}.

Thus, the maximum amount you can contribute is: {{ Constants.endowment }} + {{ cumulative_payoff }} = {{ max_amount }}.
Your contribution can be any number from 0 to {{ max_amount }}.



Payoffs

The earnings from the project are calculated as follows:
The contributions of all {{ Constants.players_per_group }} participants are added up, and are multiplied by a factor of {{ Constants.multiplier }}. The resulting amount is the total earnings from the project, which is evenly split among all {{ Constants.players_per_group }} players.

Your payoff equals your earnings from the project, plus the amount you did not contribute.



Examples

Here are three examples of how individual contributions lead to payouts.

  1. All players contribute the maximum amount.
    In this case, the common pot will be {{ max_amount }} + {{ max_amount }} + {{ max_amount }} = {{ example1_sum }}.
    As described, the pot will be multiplied by {{ Constants.multiplier }}, and then split equally.

    Thus, in this case everyone will receive: ({{ example1_sum }} * {{ Constants.multiplier }}) : 3 = {{ example1_final }}

  2. Two players contribute the maximum amount, and one player doesn't contribute anything at all.
    In this case, the common pot will be {{ max_amount }} + {{ max_amount }} + 0 = {{ example2_sum }}.
    Again, the pot will be multiplied by {{ Constants.multiplier }}, and then split equally.

    Thus, the players who contributed the maximum amount will receive: ({{ example2_sum }} * {{ Constants.multiplier }}) : 3 = {{ example2_final }}
    The player who did not contribute anything, however, will receive: {{ max_amount }} + {{ example2_final }} = {{ example2_p3 }}.

  3. No player contributes anything.
    If no player contributes anything, payout is simple. There is nothing in the common pot, so there is nothing to multiply and distribute.
    Thus, all players simply keep what they had earned so far: {{max_amount}}.
